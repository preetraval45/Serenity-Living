Who We Are

About Serenity Living

Serenity Living is a senior living and nursing community dedicated to providing compassionate, dignified care in a peaceful environment.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, engaging, and nurturing home where residents live with dignity and purpose. We partner closely with families to deliver consistent, compassionate care.

Our Values

  • Compassion and respect in every interaction
  • Personalized care plans rooted in clinical excellence
  • Connection through community, activities, and family
  • Safety, comfort, and a sense of belonging

What Families Can Expect

Clear communication, thoughtful activities, and a professional team that treats your loved one like family. From medication support to daily enrichment, we attend to every detail.

  • Daily wellness checks and care coordination
  • Chef-prepared meals and nutrition support
  • Housekeeping, laundry, and maintenance
  • Licensed, experienced caregivers on site 24/7
  • Personalized care plans and medication management
  • Secure community with memory care support
  • Chef-prepared meals with special diet options
  • Daily enrichment: wellness classes, arts, music, and outings
  • Family-first communication and care coordination
What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive care options tailored to each resident’s needs, delivered with compassion and respect.

Assisted Living

Support for daily activities while preserving independence.

  • Bathing, dressing, grooming
  • Medication reminders
  • Housekeeping & laundry

Skilled Nursing

24/7 licensed nursing with care coordination and monitoring.

  • Post-acute care
  • Care plan oversight
  • Vitals & monitoring

Memory Care

Secure, compassionate care focused on routine and familiarity.

  • Structured days
  • Secure neighborhood
  • Family updates

Respite Care

Short-term stays for recovery or family caregiver relief.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Rehab coordination
  • All-inclusive stay

Rehabilitation & Therapy

Physical, occupational, and speech therapy to restore function.

  • Goal-based plans
  • Licensed therapists
  • Progress tracking

Dining & Nutrition

Seasonal menus crafted with balanced, nutritious choices.

  • Dietary accommodations
  • Chef-prepared meals
  • Consult with nutritionist
